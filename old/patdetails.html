Primary Recommendation

Acetaminophen + Rest + Fluids

For relief of fever, headache, and general discomfort

Dosage

Acetaminophen 500mg, 1-2 tablets every 6 hours as needed (not to exceed 4000mg in 24 hours)

Instructions
  • Take with food or milk to reduce stomach upset
  • Rest as much as possible
  • Drink plenty of fluids (water, clear broths, or electrolyte solutions)
  • Use a humidifier or take steamy showers to help with congestion
Expected Outcome

Symptoms should begin to improve within 24-48 hours. Fever and headache relief typically occurs within 30-60 minutes after taking acetaminophen.

When to Seek Medical Care

Contact a healthcare provider immediately if you experience any of the following:

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent chest pain or pressure
  • Fever above 103°F (39.4°C) that doesn't respond to medication
  • Severe headache with confusion or altered mental status
  • Severe persistent vomiting
