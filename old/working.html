Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our personalized medicine approach.

How is EvoRX different from traditional healthcare?

Unlike traditional one-size-fits-all approaches, EvoRX uses your unique genetic profile, medical history, and lifestyle factors to create a truly personalized treatment plan that's optimized specifically for you.

Is genetic testing required?

While genetic testing provides valuable insights that enhance personalization, it's not mandatory. We can still create an effective personalized plan based on your medical history, current health status, and lifestyle factors.

How long does it take to see results?

Many patients report noticeable improvements within the first 4-6 weeks of starting their personalized treatment plan. However, the timeline can vary depending on your specific condition and health goals.

Is EvoRX covered by insurance?

We work with many major insurance providers, and coverage varies depending on your specific plan. Our team will help you understand your coverage options before you begin.

How secure is my health data?

We take data security extremely seriously. All your health information is protected with bank-level encryption and complies with HIPAA regulations. We never share your data without your explicit consent.
